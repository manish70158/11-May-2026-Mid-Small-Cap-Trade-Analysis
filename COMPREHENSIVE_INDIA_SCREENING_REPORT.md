## 🔍 Detailed Screening Methodology

### Stage 1: Valuation Filters (Max 30 points)

**Criteria:**

1. **P/E Ratio vs Sector (10 points)**
   - Discount ≥20% from sector average
   - Avoid P/E < 5 (value traps)
   - Score: Proportional to discount

2. **P/B + ROE Combo (10 points)**
   - P/B < 3 AND ROE > 15%
   - Rewards efficient capital use at discount
   - Score: Function of (ROE/15) × (3-PB)/3

3. **PEG Ratio (10 points)**
   - Target: PEG < 1
   - Growth at reasonable/cheap price
   - Score: Higher for lower PEG

**Minimum Threshold:** 10 points to advance

---

### Stage 2: Growth Assessment (Max 40 points)

**Criteria:**

1. **Historical Growth (15 points)**
   - Revenue growth >20% YoY
   - Earnings growth >25% YoY
   - Consistency check

2. **Technical Momentum (10 points)**
   - RSI 40-60 (balanced)
   - Volume analysis
   - Accumulation phase

3. **Business Quality (15 points)**
   - Profit margins
   - ROE strength
   - Competitive position

**Minimum Threshold:** 15 points to advance

---

### Stage 3: Risk Screening (Max 30 points)

**Criteria:**

1. **Financial Health (10 points)**
   - Debt-to-Equity < 1 (< 0.5 for smallcaps)
   - Exception: NBFCs/Banks where leverage is business model
   - Interest coverage > 3x
   - Current ratio > 1.2

2. **Liquidity (10 points)**
   - Daily volume > 500K (midcap), > 200K (smallcap)
   - Market cap > ₹5,000 Cr (midcap), > ₹1,000 Cr (smallcap)
   - Avoid circuit-prone stocks

3. **Volatility (10 points)**
   - Beta 0.8-1.5 (moderate)
   - Max drawdown < 40%
   - Lower = better

**Minimum Threshold:** 15 points to advance

## 📉 Stocks That Didn't Make the Cut

### Common Rejection Reasons:

1. **Failed Valuation (Expensive)**
   - High P/E without corresponding growth
   - P/B > 3 with ROE < 15%
   - PEG ratio > 2

2. **Failed Growth (Stagnant)**
   - Revenue growth < 20%
   - Declining earnings
   - Deteriorating margins
   - Poor technical setup (RSI extremes)

3. **Failed Risk (Too Risky)**
   - Debt-to-Equity > 1 (non-financials)
   - Low liquidity (< 200K daily volume)
   - High volatility (beta > 1.5, drawdown > 40%)
   - Small market cap with poor float

### Notable Exclusions:

- **IT Services Stocks:** Generally trading at high valuations (P/E 25-30+)
- **Consumer Staples:** Low growth, high valuations
